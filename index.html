  • Step into the world of ancient Egypt, where gods walk among mortals and every shadow hides a myth. In Kemet’s tales, you and your team can enter five living legends, from Isis’s scorpion‑guarded journey to Horus’s battle for the throne. Solve puzzles drawn directly from hieroglyphic texts, make moral choices that change your fate, and restore balance to the Two Lands. Will you escape before chaos consumes you? Each room is a story. R.1: The Shipwrecked Sailor – Small (20 min)

Description:
You are a sailor on a magical island. A giant golden serpent blocks your escape. Solve its riddle or stay forever.
Difficulty: Easy

R.2: The Blinding of Truth by Falsehood – Small (20 min)

Description:
Your mother is Ma’at, goddess of truth. She was blinded by her brother Falsehood. You stand in a courtroom. Three daggers. Three witnesses. One lie. Find the real blade. Escape before the false verdict locks you in.
Difficulty: Easy to Medium

R.3:The Destruction of Mankind – Medium (35 min)

Description:
Sekhmet is hunting humanity. Ra’s only plan: make her drink beer dyed red as blood. You are the scribe. Mix the brew, flood the room, and put the lion to sleep.
Difficulty: Medium

R.4: The Tale of Two Brothers – Medium (35 min)

Description:
You are Bata, falsely accused by your brother’s wife. Anpu is coming with a knife. A cow, a spindle, and an acacia tree hold your only proof. Escape or die.
Difficulty: Medium to Hard

R.5: The Contendings of Horus and Set Large – (55 min)

Description:
The throne of Egypt has no king. You are Horus’s champion. Three trials: stone boat, false lettuce, golden scales. Win them all. Claim the crown. End the chaos.
Difficulty: Hard
